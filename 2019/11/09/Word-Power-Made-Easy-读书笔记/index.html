Word Power Made Easy 读书笔记

Posted on 2019-11-09
Words count in article: | Reading time ≈

《Word Power Made Easy》这本书我已经看完了session 2，一定要说下我的感受。首先，书本小巧，只比手掌大一寸，但是知识丰富，其次行文流畅，远没有上学时期阅读文章带来的机械感，再次层次循序渐进，越读越想读下去，可以另外找笔记本来记录学习过程，也可以记在在线笔记本上，便于随时温习。

书籍的相关评价可以参考这里

  • https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/827597.Word_Power_Made_Easy
  • https://www.flipkart.com/word-power-made-easy/p/itmfc4zjwegzgkkh
  • https://www.amazon.in/Word-Power-Made-Norman-Lewis/dp/8183071007

不论母语本身就是英语的人，还是语言学习者，都对这本书作了很高的评价，对于我们刚刚开始想要提高英语水平的人，是一个不错的阅读选择，我自身也已经开始阅读这本书，到此时已经学完了前两个session。

下面列举出前两节中的重要词汇

  1. egoist 利己主义者
    • a self-centered or selfish person (opposed to altruist).
    • an arrogantly conceited person; egotist.
    • an adherent of the metaphysical principle of the ego, or self; solipsist
  1. egotist 自我主义者
  • a conceited, boastful person.
  • a selfish person; egoist.
  1. altruist 利他主义者
  • a person unselfishly concerned for or devoted to the welfare of others (opposed to egoist).
  1. introvert 个性内向者
  • a shy person.
  • Psychology. a person characterized by concern primarily with his or her own thoughts and feelings (opposed to extrovert).
  • Zoology. a part that is or can be introverted.
  1. extrovert 个性外向的
  • an outgoing, gregarious person.
  • Psychology. a person characterized by extroversion; a person concerned primarily with the physical and social environment (opposed to introvert).
  1. ambivert 中间性格
  • one whose personality type is intermediate between extrovert and introvert.
  1. misanthrope 愤世嫉俗者
  • a hater of humankind.
  1. misogynist 厌恶女人的人
  • a person who hates, dislikes, mistrusts, or mistreats women.
  1. misogamist 厌恶婚姻的人
  • hatred of marriage.
  1. ascetic 禁欲者， 苦行憎
  • characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.
